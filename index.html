Playlist Skins

Turn any Spotify playlist into a retro boombox skin.

1. Drop your Spotify playlist

Paste a normal Spotify playlist link. The app will turn it into an embed URL automatically.

2. Preview your skin

This version uses your repo image files directly: BoomBox.png and PurpleRabbit.png.
3. Copy this HTML widget to use on your site
Paste this into any site that allows custom HTML, like Webador or WordPress.
