Playlist Skins Privacy Policy

Playlist Skins does not collect, store, or sell personal user data.

This app allows users to paste music links and generate custom visual player skins.

Third-party music services such as Spotify, YouTube, SoundCloud, Apple Music, or Amazon Music may load embedded content and may use their own cookies, tracking, or privacy practices according to their own policies.

Playlist Skins does not require account registration and does not directly store private personal information submitted by users.
